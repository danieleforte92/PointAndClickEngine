# Governance

Point & Click Engine is maintainer-led during Creator Alpha. Maintainers decide
the release scope, merge changes, resolve conduct concerns, and make final
compatibility and redistribution decisions. Contributors are encouraged to
propose changes through issues and pull requests; substantial direction changes
should be discussed before implementation.

Release approval is intentionally human-led. Automation supplies tests,
provenance reports, checksums, and candidate artifacts, but does not substitute
for rights review, code-signing authority, provider-terms decisions, or a
manual packaged-preview decision.
